who we are ?

Green Future Entrepreneurship aid is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that provides financial support to young entrepreneurs in Ghana and is expanding its services to other African countries. Their goal is to assist young entrepreneurs who want to start a business but lack the necessary capital. They offer funding through their GFtoken program. This initiative aims to empower and support aspiring entrepreneurs in realizing their business dreams.
